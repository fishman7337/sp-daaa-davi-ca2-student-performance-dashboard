# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project should remain respectful, fair, and safe for academic collaboration. Contributors are expected to:

- Communicate with care and assume good faith.
- Give constructive feedback focused on the work.
- Respect privacy, academic integrity, and institutional policies.
- Avoid harassment, insults, discrimination, or public sharing of sensitive data.
- Credit collaborators and sources accurately.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, intimidation, discriminatory comments, deliberate misrepresentation of work, publishing private information without consent, and using project data outside its approved academic purpose.

## Reporting

For academic or conduct concerns, contact the project maintainers through the normal course or institutional channels. Security-specific concerns should follow `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove inappropriate content, decline contributions, restrict repository access, or escalate matters through Singapore Polytechnic processes when needed.
